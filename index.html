** RULES :
Pick your starter Pokemon.
This will be your Pokemon for the rest of the game.
Every time you attack, your attack power will permanently grow by the base Attack power, but you will never be able to regenerate health.
Your opponent will always do damage based on their Counter Attack power and it will stay stagnant throughout the game.
There are no elemental advantages, so choose your characters wisely and good luck!
